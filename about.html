About LOPARK

The mission of a LOPARK - smart parking system using LoRa (Long Range) technology is to optimize the utilization of parking spaces by providing real-time data on available parking spots to drivers. The system is designed to reduce the time and effort required to find parking spaces, improve traffic flow, and reduce emissions caused by vehicles searching for parking.we are providing the Lopark to enhance the efficiency and convenience of parking while reducing congestion, emissions, and frustration for drivers.
