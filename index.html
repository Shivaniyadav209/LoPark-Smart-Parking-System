LOPARK- YOUR OWN PARKING APP

LOPARK- A smart parking system is an advanced parking management solution that leverages modern technologies to provide an efficient and automated parking experience. With a smart parking system ,drivers can use mobile apps, websites to search for available parking spaces, book them in advance, and even pay for the parking electronically.... The system can also help reduce congestion and improve traffic flow by directing drivers to available parking spaces.

Features of LOPARK

Our system can allow parking operators to remotely monitor parking areas and detect any issues, such as parking violations, maintenance needs, or security concerns.Also our system is be designed to operate on low power and use energy-efficient devices, reducing the overall energy consumption and environmental impact of the parking system.

Services provided:

  • Provide real time information about the parking spaces.
  • The system can enable automated payment processing.
  • The system can provide enhanced security features.
