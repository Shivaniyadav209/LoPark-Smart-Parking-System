Services Offered By LOPARK

    REAL TIME PARKING AVAILABILITY

  • Real-time parking availability is a service offered by a smart parking system using LoRa technology that enables drivers to quickly and easily find an available parking space. This service works by using sensors that are installed in each parking space to detect the occupancy status. The sensors are connected to a LoRaWAN gateway, which collects and transmits the data to the central server.
  • The central server processes the data and provides real-time parking availability information to drivers through a mobile app or a website. This information can include the number of available parking spaces in a particular area, the location of available parking spaces, and the type of parking space available (e.g., handicap accessible, EV charging, etc.)
  • By providing real-time parking availability information, drivers can save time and reduce stress by avoiding the frustration of searching for a parking space. They can also reduce their carbon footprint by reducing the time and fuel wasted in searching for a parking space. Parking operators can also benefit from this service by optimizing the use of parking spaces, reducing congestion, and increasing revenue.

    • Overall, real-time parking availability is a key service offered by a smart parking system using LoRa technology that can improve the parking experience for drivers and increase efficiency for parking operators.

    PARKING GUIDANCE

  • Parking guidance is a service offered by a smart parking system using LoRa technology that helps drivers find available parking spaces quickly and easily. This service uses a combination of sensors, LoRaWAN gateways, and a central server to provide real-time information on parking availability and guidance on how to get to an available parking space.
  • The parking guidance service works by installing sensors in each parking space to detect the occupancy status. The sensors are connected to a LoRaWAN gateway, which collects and transmits the data to the central server. The central server processes the data and provides real-time parking availability information to drivers through a mobile app or a website.
  • In addition to real-time parking availability information, the parking guidance service can also provide turn-by-turn directions to an available parking space. This is achieved by using GPS and mapping data to guide drivers to the nearest available parking space. The directions can be displayed on a mobile app or an in-car navigation system, providing a seamless and stress-free parking experience.

    AUTOMATED PAYMENTS

  • Automated payments is a service offered by a smart parking system using LoRa technology that enables drivers to pay for parking without having to interact with a payment terminal or an attendant. This service uses a combination of sensors, LoRaWAN gateways, and a central server to detect when a vehicle enters and exits a parking space and to calculate the parking fee based on the duration of stay.
  • The automated payments service works by installing sensors in each parking space to detect the occupancy status. The sensors are connected to a LoRaWAN gateway, which collects and transmits the data to the central server. When a vehicle enters a parking space, the sensor detects the occupancy status and starts a timer. When the vehicle exits the parking space, the sensor detects the vacancy status and stops the timer. The central server then calculates the parking fee based on the duration of stay and other factors, such as the type of parking space and any special promotions or discounts.
  • Drivers can pay for parking using a mobile app or a payment terminal. The mobile app can be linked to a credit card or a prepaid account, and the parking fee can be deducted automatically. The payment terminal can accept cash or credit card payments, and it can issue a receipt or a ticket.

    • By providing automated payments, a smart parking system using LoRa technology can reduce the time and hassle associated with paying for parking. It can also reduce the need for on-site personnel to collect payment and issue tickets, which can lower operating costs for parking operators.

    MAINTAINANCE AND MONITORING

  • Maintenance and monitoring is a service offered by a smart parking system using LoRa technology that enables parking operators to remotely monitor the performance and status of the system and perform maintenance tasks as needed. This service uses a combination of sensors, LoRaWAN gateways, and a central server to collect and analyze data on the system's performance and identify potential issues before they become problems.
  • The maintenance and monitoring service works by installing sensors in each parking space to detect the occupancy status. The sensors are connected to a LoRaWAN gateway, which collects and transmits the data to the central server. The central server can then analyze the data to identify trends, patterns, and potential issues that may require maintenance or repair.
  • The maintenance and monitoring service can also provide real-time alerts to parking operators if there are any system failures, such as a sensor malfunction or a communication outage. This can help operators quickly identify and resolve issues, reducing downtime and minimizing disruptions to the parking system.
  • In addition to monitoring the performance of the sensors and gateways, the maintenance and monitoring service can also monitor the status of the payment system, the mobile app, and other components of the parking system. This can help operators ensure that the system is functioning as expected and identify any issues that may be impacting the user experience.

    • By providing maintenance and monitoring, a smart parking system using LoRa technology can improve the reliability and uptime of the system, reduce the need for on-site maintenance personnel, and minimize the impact of downtime on users. This can ultimately lead to a better overall parking experience for drivers and increase revenue for parking operators.
